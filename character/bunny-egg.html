🗺️ Best Maps

Bunny Egg is strongest on maps with lots of vertical sections:

  • Candy Arena — S-tier, double jump helps dodge obstacles
  • Frozen Peak — A-tier, ice climbs much easier with 2 jumps
  • Volcano Rush — B-tier, lava gaps cleared with good timing

🎮 Best Mode

Race Mode — Best pick for vertical obstacle races.

Survival: ⭐⭐⭐⭐ (air mobility helps dodge)

Team Mode: ⭐⭐⭐ (decent support)

📝 Overview

Bunny Egg is the most agile starter character. With above-average jump height and a double-jump-like air control, Bunny Egg excels on vertically challenging maps. The fluffy exterior hides a surprisingly competitive pick for advanced players.

🎯 How to Unlock

Unlocked at Level 3. Every player gets Bunny Egg after their first few matches.

💡 Pro Tips

  • Bunny Egg's air control lets you slightly change direction mid-jump — use this to correct landings on narrow platforms.
  • Bunny Egg pairs well with Candy Arena and Frozen Peak where vertical movement is key.
  • Don't underestimate Bunny Egg in Survival mode — the extra air mobility helps dodge knockback attacks.
