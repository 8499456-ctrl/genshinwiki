📝 Overview

Dragon Egg is a limited-event character with a unique passive: after dashing, Dragon Egg leaves a short flame trail that lasts 1.5 seconds. Opponents who step in the trail are briefly slowed. This area-denial ability makes Dragon Egg a menace in tight corridors and chokepoints.

🎯 How to Unlock

Limited character — was available during Season 3: Dragon's Fury event. May return in future events or shop rotations.

💡 Pro Tips

  • Use Dragon Egg's flame trail at chokepoints to block pursuers in Race mode.
  • In Survival mode, lay down flame trails near edges to discourage opponents from approaching.
  • Dragon Egg's low jump makes it weak on vertical maps — pick accordingly.
