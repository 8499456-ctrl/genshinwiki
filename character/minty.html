📝 Overview

Minty is a speed-focused egg with the fastest dash cooldown in the game at just 4 seconds. The trade-off is lower jump height, making Minty less effective on vertical-heavy maps. But on flat or platform-heavy courses, Minty's rapid dashes create unstoppable momentum.

🎯 How to Unlock

Reach Level 10 in any mode, or purchase for 1,500 gems from the Character Shop.

💡 Pro Tips

  • Chain your dashes — Minty's 4s cooldown means you can dash every few platforms.
  • Avoid vertical maps like Candy Arena where Minty's low jump becomes a liability.
  • Minty is S-tier on Volcano Rush — the flat lava platforms are perfect for dash chaining.
