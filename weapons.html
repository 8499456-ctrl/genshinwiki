💡 Weapon Selection Principles
  • Prioritize Crit Rate/Crit DMG substats — Best for DPS chars. Aim for 60/120 baseline.
  • You can never have too many ER weapons — Support characters are always hungry for ER. Favonius series is evergreen.
  • R5 4-star > R1 5-star — Max refinement 4-star is often better than R1 5-star (especially Sacrificial/The Catch).
  • Battle Pass weapons are great value — Serpent Spine/Ballad of the Fjords/Solar Pearl/Wolf-Fang are all strong.
  • Don't underestimate craftable weapons — Sapwood Blade/Amenoma Kageuchi/Prototype Amber/Whiteblind.
  • Even 3-star weapons have hidden gems — Slingshot (Tighnari), Black Tassel (HP Zhongli).
