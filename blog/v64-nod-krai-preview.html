❄️ Version 6.4 "Whispers of Nod-Krai" — Everything We Know

📅 July 20, 2026 · 👤 Wiki · ⏱ 7 min read
Home › Blog › v6.4 Nod-Krai Preview
v6.4 Nod-Krai Preview Skirk Dainsleif

HoYoverse has officially unveiled the first details for Version 6.4 "Whispers of Nod-Krai", dropping in early August 2026. After the Moon of Four conclusion, we're heading north — into the icy frontier between Snezhnaya and the unknown. Here's everything we know so far.

⚠️ Speculation Note: This article compiles official teasers, beta leaks, and credible datamines. Final details may change before the live patch.

🗺️ New Region: Nod-Krai (First Zone)

Nod-Krai has been name-dropped since the early Archon Quests — a frozen wasteland at the northern edge of Teyvat, bordering Snezhnaya. Version 6.4 introduces the first explorable zone, an area about the size of Chenyu Vale.

  • Themes: Eternal twilight, aurora skies, ancient ruins buried under permafrost
  • New mechanics: "Rimewalk" — a traversal ability that lets you glide across ice sheets at high speed
  • World Quests: Heavily tied to the Hexenzirkel and the history of the Second Who Came
  • New Domains: One artifact domain (two new sets) and one talent material domain

⚔️ Dainsleif Quest: Act II — "The Serpent's Bargain"

This is the big one. Over two years since the last Dainsleif quest "Caribert," we finally get the continuation. According to leaks, this quest delves into:

  • The truth behind the Sinner Khaenri'ah
  • Dainsleif's past with the other Twilight Swordsmen
  • A boss fight against a corrupted Field Tiller (giant ruin machine) in a flashback sequence
  • Major lore revelations about the Abyss Order's true leadership
💡 Pro Tip: Complete "Caribert" (Archon Quest Interlude Chapter) before 6.4 drops. The quest chain features a recommended Lv. 90 party — bring strong single-target DPS for the boss.

🌟 New Characters

Skirk — 5-Star Cryo Sword

Role: Main DPS / On-Field Carry

Fans have waited years for Skirk to become playable. Tartaglia's master finally steps into the spotlight with a unique mechanic: "Void Blade" — after using her Elemental Burst, Skirk enters a empowered state where her normal attacks deal coordinated Cryo damage and ignore 30% of enemy DEF.

  • Elemental Skill: Creates an Abyssal rift that pulses Cryo damage and marks enemies
  • Elemental Burst: 80-energy, massive AoE Cryo slash + Void Blade state for 10s
  • Passive: Team Cryo DMG bonus based on Skirk's ATK
  • Best teammates: Shenhe, Kazuha or Xilonen, Furina

Pyrrha — 4-Star Pyro Polearm (Sub-DPS)

A fiery fighter from Natlan's Scions of the Canopy. Pyrrha provides off-field Pyro application and a team ATK buff when allies trigger Burning.

  • Verdict: Strong free option for Kinich and Emilie teams
  • How to get: Free from 6.4 event

🎁 Events in 6.4

  • ✧ Frostlit Tournament — Main flagship event. Combat challenge with Cryo buffs. Free 4-star character: Pyrrha
  • ✧ Aurora Photography — Exploration event in Nod-Krai. Take photos of aurora phenomena. Rewards: primogems, talent books, namecard
  • ✧ Rerun: Lost Riches — Find treasure-seeking seelies in Liyue and Mondstadt
  • ✧ Double Ley Line Overflow — Double rewards from Ley Line Outcrops (2x total)

🛡️ New Artifact Sets

❄️ Glacial Dominion (4.5★ to 5★)

  • 2-Piece: Cryo DMG Bonus +15%
  • 4-Piece: When a party member triggers Freeze or Superconduct, all party members gain 12% CRIT Rate and 30% CRIT DMG for 10s. Can trigger once every 12s.
  • Who needs it? Skirk (BiS), Freeze Ayaka

🌌 Echoes of the Deep (4.5★ to 5★)

  • 2-Piece: Energy Recharge +20%
  • 4-Piece: After using Elemental Burst, the equipping character's next Normal Attack deals 120% ATK as True Damage (ignores DEF). Triggerable once every 6s.
  • Who needs it? Support characters who snapshot after burst (Xilonen, Yelan, etc.)

💬 Final Thoughts

Version 6.4 is shaping up to be one of the most lore-heavy patches since 5.0. Between Nod-Krai's debut, Skirk's long-awaited release, and Act 2 of Dainsleif's quest, this is a patch where story fans win big.

If you've been saving primogems, this is the patch to spend them. Skirk looks like a meta-defining Cryo carry, and with Shenhe + Wrio reruns, Cryo teams are about to have their best era since 3.x.

Start saving those frosty primogems now, Travelers ❄️
