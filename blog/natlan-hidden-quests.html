Natlan Hidden Quests Exploration Primogems

Natlan is full of hidden world quests, secret mechanisms, and treasure puzzles that aren't marked on the map. This guide covers every hidden quest discovered so far in v6.3, with step-by-step instructions and reward breakdowns.

Total potential rewards from hidden quests in Natlan: ~1,200 Primogems, 8+ Crowns of Insight materials, 4 exclusive namecards, and 2 hidden achievements.

🔥 Hidden Quest 1: The Echo of Scorched Stone

📍 Location: Tezcatepetonco Range — South Cave
🎁 Rewards: 40 Primogems, 2 Hero's Wit, 1 Hidden Achievement

How to start: Go to the cave entrance at the southern cliff of Tezcatepetonco Range. You'll find a broken tablet and an NPC named "Old Khuaj."

Steps:

  1. Talk to Old Khuaj to learn about the three lost echoes
  2. Echo 1: Light the four torches in the cave using pyro (order doesn't matter)
  3. Echo 2: Climb to the top of the waterfall west of the cave and use any Nightsoul character to resonate with the crystal
  4. Echo 3: Defeat the Lava Primal Construct south of the PoC statue
  5. Return to Old Khuaj to receive the rewards

Hidden achievement: "The Echo Returns" — 10 Primogems

🌋 Hidden Quest 2: The Sleeping Flame

📍 Location: Chaskan Coppe — Underground Lava Chamber
🎁 Rewards: 60 Primogems, 3 Sanctifying Unction, 1 Namecard

How to start: Near the seven-statue waypoint in Chaskan Coppe, break the cracked wall on the cliff side. Fall down to find a sealed chamber with a giant flame motif.

Steps:

  1. Interact with all 5 flame pillars in the chamber
  2. Each pillar corresponds to a different element: Pyro, Hydro, Electro, Dendro, Cryo
  3. Activate them in the order: Pyro → Dendro → Electro → Hydro → Cryo (based on the clues on the wall)
  4. A Precious Chest spawns after the correct sequence
  5. Open the chest and read the note inside to complete the quest

Namecard: "Natlan — Flame Sigil" — exclusive to this quest

🦎 Hidden Quest 3: The Lizardfolk's Offering

📍 Location: Coatepec Mountain — Saurian Nest
🎁 Rewards: 50 Primogems, 3 Teachings of Contention, Mystic Enhancement Ore ×8

How to start: In the Saurian nest at Coatepec Mountain, there's a hidden cave behind the waterfall. Inside is an offering altar and three Saurian statues.

Steps:

  1. Collect 3 kinds of regional specialty fruits nearby
  2. Place each fruit on the corresponding altar
  3. Defeat the wave of Fungus that spawn
  4. Talk to the invisible Saurian spirit (use elemental sight to find it)
  5. Receive the offering chest

💎 Hidden Quest 4: The Forgotten Warriors

📍 Location: Ancient Battlefield — West of Stadium of Sacred Flame
🎁 Rewards: 80 Primogems, 1 Crown of Insight, 1 Hidden Achievement

How to start: At the ancient battlefield, interact with 5 ghostly warrior spirits scattered across the area. Use elemental sight to locate them.

Steps:

  1. Warrior 1: Near the broken chariot — gives a quest item "Shield Fragment"
  2. Warrior 2: Beside the collapsed pillar — gives "Sword Fragment"
  3. Warrior 3: At the center campfire — gives "Helmet Fragment"
  4. Warrior 4: Climb the watchtower — gives "Arrowhead Fragment"
  5. Warrior 5: In the underground bunker entrance — gives "Banner Fragment"
  6. Assemble all fragments at the central altar to summon the final boss
  7. Boss: Spectral Warrior King (level 95, takes reduced physical damage)

Hidden achievement: "Ancient Legacy" — 15 Primogems

Tip: Bring a hydro + dendro team for this fight. The boss is weak to bloom reactions.

✨ Hidden Quest 5: The Star-Gazing Sage

📍 Location: Ameyalco Waters — Island Tower
🎁 Rewards: 40 Primogems, 1 Namecard, Teleport Waypoint unlock

How to start: Find the abandoned tower on the small island east of Ameyalco Waters. Read the journal at the base of the tower.

Steps:

  1. Climb the tower and adjust the 3 telescopes to face specific constellations
  2. Constellation 1 (East): The Dragon — align to the brightest star cluster
  3. Constellation 2 (South): The Flame — align to the reddish stars
  4. Constellation 3 (West): The Shield — align to the formation near the mountain
  5. Return to the journal to receive a hidden waypoint and the namecard

Namecard: "Natlan — Stargazer" — one of the rarest in the game
