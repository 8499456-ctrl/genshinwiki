Skirk Build v6.4 Cryo Guide

After years of waiting, Skirk — Tartaglia's mysterious master — is finally playable in Version 6.4 "Whispers of Nod-Krai". As a 5-star Cryo Sword main DPS, she brings a unique "Void Blade" mechanic that shreds through enemy defenses. Here's everything you need to build her optimally.

TL;DR: Skirk is a Cryo on-field carry who excels in Freeze and Mono-Cryo teams. Prioritize CRIT > ATK% > ER. Her signature weapon "Void Edge" is her best-in-slot by a wide margin.

🔍 Kit Overview

Before we dive into builds, here's a quick refresher on Skirk's abilities:

AbilityDescription
Normal Attack5-hit Cryo sword combo. Decent multipliers but not her primary damage source outside of Void Blade state.
Elemental SkillCreates an Abyssal Rift that pulses Cryo DMG over 8s and marks enemies. Marked enemies take 15% increased Cryo DMG from all sources.
Elemental Burst80 energy. Massive AoE Cryo slash, then enters Void Blade state for 10s. In this state: normal attacks deal coordinated Cryo DMG, ignore 30% DEF, and apply faster Cryo.
A1 PassiveWhen Skirk triggers a reaction on a marked enemy, she gains 8% CRIT Rate for 8s. Stacks up to 3 times (24% total).
A4 PassiveTeam gains 15% Cryo DMG Bonus when Skirk is in Void Blade state.

🛡️ Best Artifacts

❄️ Glacial Dominion (New 6.4 Domain) — BiS

  • 2-Piece: Cryo DMG +15%
  • 4-Piece: When any party member triggers Freeze or Superconduct, all gain 12% CRIT Rate and 30% CRIT DMG for 10s. Triggers once per 12s.
  • Verdict: Tailor-made for Skirk. The proc lines up perfectly with her Burst window.

❄️ Blizzard Strayer — Strong Alternative

  • 2-Piece: Cryo DMG +15%
  • 4-Piece: +40% CRIT Rate against Frozen enemies. Works best in freeze teams.
  • Verdict: If you already have a god-rolled Blizzard set from Ayaka days, it's competitive with Glacial Dominion in freeze teams.

2-Piece Mixes

If you're prefarming: 2x Glacial Dominion / 2x ATK% (Gladiator/Shimenawa).

👥 Best Team Compositions

❄️ Premium Freeze — Skirk's Strongest Team

  • Skirk — Main DPS
  • Shenhe — Cryo battery, quills, Cryo RES shred
  • Furina — Hydro app, team-wide DMG buff, enables freeze
  • Xilonen (Cinder City) — RES shred, healing, 40% Elemental DMG from Scroll set

Rotation: Furina E+Q → Xilonen E+NA+Q → Shenhe E+Q → Skirk E+Q (Void Blade combo)

💧 Mono-Cryo + Hydro Flex

  • Skirk — Main DPS
  • Kazuha — VV shred, Cryo DMG bonus, grouping
  • Kokomi / Barbara (TTDS) — Healing, Hydro app, freeze enabler
  • Shenhe / Rosaria (C6) — Battery + Cryo buff

Alternative for F2P: Replace Shenhe with Rosaria or Kaeya (high investment). Replace Furina with Xingqiu.

🔥 Reverse Melt (Fun Budget Option)

  • Skirk — Main DPS
  • Xiangling — Off-field Pyro app
  • Bennett — ATK buff + Pyro resonance + healing
  • Kazuha / Sucrose — VV shred

Note: Skirk loses her A4 passive in this comp (no freeze), but the raw numbers can still clear Abyss floors 9-11 easily.

🔢 Talent Priority

  1. Elemental Burst — Crown (primary damage source + Void Blade state)
  2. Elemental Skill — Crown (mark DMG + team buff uptime)
  3. Normal Attack — Level 8 (only matters during Void Blade)

💬 Final Thoughts

Skirk is shaping up to be a top-tier Cryo carry who brings something fresh: a DEF-ignoring mechanic that makes her effective even against high-HP bosses. In Freeze teams, she's comfortable and strong. In Mono-Cryo, she outperforms Ayaka in sustained damage, though Ayaka still wins in burst windows.

Should you pull? If you need a Cryo DPS — absolutely. If you already have Ayaka C2+, she's a sidegrade in freeze but an upgrade everywhere else. Her animations are stunning, her lore payoff is massive, and she's genuinely fun to play.
