Genshin Impact 5.0 — Everything We Know

📅 July 18, 2026 · 📁 Natlan · ⏱ 4 min read

Version 5.0 is bringing us to the long-awaited Pyro region of Natlan. Here's a roundup of everything officially confirmed so far.

New Characters

The 5.0 cycle introduces three new characters:

  • Mavuika — 5★ Pyro Claymore, Archon of Natlan. Support/DPS hybrid with unique Nightsoul mechanics.
  • Iansan — 4★ Electro Polearm, coach-like support that boosts team ATK and movement speed.
  • Xilonen — 5★ Geo Sword, a blacksmith artisan with resistance shred and healing abilities.

Pyro Traveler

After three years, the Traveler finally unlocks Pyro. The kit features:

  • Elemental Skill — Throw a blazing ring that marks enemies, dealing AoE Pyro damage over time
  • Elemental Burst — Large AoE Pyro eruption that leaves a field boosting team Pyro DMG by 25%
  • Exploration passive — Reduce stamina consumption in Natlan by 20%

💡 Tip: Pyro Traveler works exceptionally well with Furina and Bennett, forming a strong mono-Pyro core for Abyss.

New Region: Natlan

Natlan introduces several new gameplay mechanics:

  • Nightsoul's Blessing — A Natlan-exclusive state that boosts character abilities and enables unique movement mechanics like wall-running and lava surfing
  • Phlogiston — New renewable resource used for Nightsoul abilities in the open world
  • Graffiti-based exploration — Solve ancient mural puzzles to unlock hidden domains and rewards

Release Date

Version 5.0 is expected to launch on August 28, 2026, with Mavuika's banner running in the first phase alongside a Chronicled Wish rerun.
