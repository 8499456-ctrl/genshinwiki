Tier List Meta v6.3 Characters

Version 6.3 "Moon of Four" brings new Natlan characters and balance shifts that change the meta landscape. Here is our updated tier list ranking every character based on Abyss performance, team versatility, and overall account value.

Note: This tier list prioritizes Abyss Floor 12 performance and team flexibility. A character in S-tier can carry teams or enable entire archetypes. C-tier characters are usable but outclassed.

📈 Biggest Risers in v6.3

  • Mualani — New Natlan DPS with Nightsoul mechanic, strong vape numbers
  • Kinich — Burning/Burgeon enabler, AOE specialist
  • Furina — Even more valuable as Natlan characters scale on team damage

📉 Biggest Fallers

  • Ayaka — Freeze continues to suffer in boss-heavy Abyss rotations
  • Wanderer — Shield dependency hurts when Abyss punishes stagger
  • Eula — Physical damage is the weakest it's ever been

🎯 Who Should You Build?

If you're a new player, prioritize S-tier 4-stars: Xingqiu, Xiangling, Bennett, Sucrose, Fischl — they will never let you down.

If you're saving for a 5-star, Furina, Kazuha, Neuvillette, and Arlecchino are your best investments for long-term account value.

💡 Reminder: A well-built A-tier team with proper synergy beats a poorly built S-tier team every time. Team composition matters more than individual character strength.
