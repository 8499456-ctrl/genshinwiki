Mualani Build Guide Natlan Hydro

Mualani is the first Natlan Hydro character, a catalyst DPS who enters Nightsoul's Blessing to deal massive single-target damage through shark-shaped charged attacks. Here's everything you need to build her.

⚡ How Mualani Works

Mualani's kit revolves around her Nightsoul's Blessing state. When she uses her skill, she enters Nightsoul mode and her normal attacks transform into shark-bite charged attacks that deal Hydro damage scaling with her max HP.

  • Skill: Enter Nightsoul state, gain movement speed, attacks consume Nightsoul points
  • Burst: Summon a giant shark that deals AOE Hydro damage based on HP
  • Passive: Increases party members' Nightsoul damage by 15%
  • C1: Extends Nightsoul duration significantly
Key mechanic: Mualani's charged attack damage is front-loaded. She spends about 6-8 seconds on-field per rotation, making her excellent for quick-swap vape teams.

🗡️ Best Weapons

⭐ 5-Star Options

  • Surf's Up (Signature) — Best in slot. CRIT Rate substat, 20% HP bonus, 40% charged attack damage boost. If you have this, use it.
  • Lost Prayer to the Sacred Winds — CRIT Rate substat, but the effect is less valuable for quick-swap play.
  • Skyward Atlas — High base ATK, but Mualani scales on HP not ATK. Still usable for the stats.

⭐ 4-Star Options

  • Widsith (R5) — Best 4-star. Random buff on swap, great for her quick-swap playstyle. CRIT DMG substat.
  • Sacrificial Fragments — EM substat helps vape damage. Skill reset is situationally useful but not ideal.
  • Frostbearer / Mappa Mare — Craftable options. Mappa Mare gives EM which is valuable for vape teams.

🛡️ Best Artifacts

🏆 4pc Golden Troupe — Best Choice

Mualani's damage is primarily from her skill (Nightsoul state), which counts as skill damage. Golden Troupe provides a massive 45% skill damage bonus at 2 stacks.

Sands: HP%
Goblet: Hydro DMG%
Circlet: CRIT Rate / CRIT DMG
Substats: CRIT > HP% > EM > ER

🏆 2pc Hydro 2pc HP — Budget Alternative

If you don't have a good Golden Troupe set, 2pc Heart of Depth + 2pc Vourukasha's Glow (or 2pc Tenacity) works fine.

  • 2pc Heart of Depth: 15% Hydro DMG
  • 2pc Vourukasha's Glow: 20% HP
  • 2pc Tenacity of the Millelith: 20% HP
Stat priority: HP% (30000+) > CRIT Rate (60%+) > CRIT DMG (160%+) > EM (100-200). Mualani does not need ATK or Energy Recharge.

🤝 Best Team Compositions

Team 1: Mualani Vape (Best Team)

Mualani + Xiangling + Kazuha + Zhongli

  • Xiangling provides consistent pyro for vape reactions
  • Kazuha groups enemies and provides 40% hydro damage bonus + VV shred
  • Zhongli gives shield + res shred for extra damage
  • Rotation: Zhongli E → Kazuha hE Q → Xiangling E Q → Mualani skill (shark bites) → burst

Team 2: Mualani Double Hydro

Mualani + Yelan + Kazuha + Bennett

  • Yelan provides hydro resonance (more HP for Mualani) and extra damage
  • Bennett heals and buffs — even though Mualani doesn't benefit from his attack buff, the heal is valuable
  • Yelan also helps with energy generation

Team 3: F2P Mualani

Mualani + Xiangling + Sucrose + Layla / Diona

  • Sucrose provides EM share and VV shred (budget Kazuha)
  • Layla or Diona provides a shield and cryo for occasional freeze
  • Less damage than premium teams but clears Abyss comfortably

🎯 Talent Priority

  1. Skill (Nightsoul shark bites) — 90% of her damage
  2. Burst (Shark summon) — Decent nuke
  3. Normal Attack — Low priority, she doesn't use normal attacks

✅ Should You Pull?

Pull Mualani if:

  • You need a strong Hydro DPS and don't have Neuvillette
  • You like front-loaded, high-number gameplay
  • You have Kazuha and Xiangling built

Skip if:

  • You already have Neuvillette or Ayato built
  • You prefer easy-to-play characters (Mualani needs some positioning skill)
  • You're saving for future Natlan characters
