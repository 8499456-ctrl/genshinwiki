🌱 New Player's Guide to Genshin Impact — What to Do First

📅 July 12, 2026 · ✍️ Wiki · 📖 8 min read
← Blog
beginnerguidetipsf2p

Just started Genshin Impact? The game gives you a massive world and very little direction. Here's the cheat sheet I wish I had on day one — no fluff, just the priorities that actually matter.

🎯 First Things First (AR 1–20)

Follow the Archon Quest

The main story is your real tutorial. It unlocks: daily commissions, domains, battle pass, and most importantly — the ability to level up your Adventure Rank past hard gates. Don't wander off exploring too much until you've at least reached Liyue in the story.

Pick Your Starter Team

You get Amber (Pyro), Kaeya (Cryo), and Lisa (Electro) for free. They're not top-tier, but they'll carry you through early content:

  • Kaeya — best early DPS, invest in him. His Cryo application is useful even in endgame
  • Lisa — great for reactions, use her Skill + Kaeya for Superconduct
  • Amber — mostly a torch-lighter, but her Baron Bunny is useful for puzzles

Don't Farm Artifacts

Artifact domains aren't worth touching until Adventure Rank 45. Before that, use whatever 3-star and 4-star pieces you get from chests and bosses. Set bonuses don't matter at low levels — just stack ATK% and Energy Recharge.

⚡ AR 20–35: Setting Up for Success

Get Xiangling for Free

Clear Spiral Abyss Floor 3, Chamber 3. You get a free Xiangling. She's one of the best 4-star characters in the entire game and remains meta years later. Build her immediately with Energy Recharge and Pyro damage.

Start Doing Daily Commissions

This is your main source of Primogems (60 per day). Combined with events, you'll earn roughly 8,000–10,000 Primogems per patch as F2P. Don't skip a single day if you care about pulling future characters.

Spend Resin Wisely

You get 180 Original Resin per day. Here's the priority order:

  1. World Bosses (ascension materials for your main DPS)
  2. Talent Books (start prefarming early)
  3. Ley Lines (Mora and EXP are always needed)
  4. Never use Fragile Resin before AR 45
💡 Save every Fragile Resin you get from rewards and Battle Pass. You'll have 40-60 by AR 45 and can spend them all on 5-star artifact domains. This single decision separates accounts that progress fast from accounts that stay stuck.

🏆 AR 35–45: Midgame Preparation

Choose Your First 5-Star Target

Don't pull on every banner. Save for one of these account-defining characters:

  • Zhongli — unbreakable shield, infinite mining, res shred
  • Nahida — best Dendro enabler, makes reaction teams viable
  • Kazuha — grouping + elemental damage bonus, fits any team
  • Furina — massive damage amp + off-field Hydro

Level the Battle Pass

Even as F2P you get the free track, which gives: 5 Acquaint Fates, 2 Fragile Resin, talent books, and weapon exp. Always reach level 50 before the patch ends — it takes about 15-20 minutes of daily play.

Explore, But Don't Burn Out

The map is huge. Don't feel pressured to 100% everything. Open chests you see along the way, collect Oculi when they're on your path, and do World Quests that are near your current story location. Leave completionist grinding for when you have nothing else to do.

🚫 Beginner Mistakes That Hurt the Most

  • ✖️ Spending Primogems on Standard Banner — only use free blue fates
  • ✖️ Building 10+ characters simultaneously — focus on 4-6, spread resources = weak team
  • ✖️ Ignoring Elemental Reactions — Vaporize and Melt do 2x damage, Aggravate scales with level
  • ✖️ Upgrading 3-star weapons past level 40 — save ore for 4-star+ weapons
  • ✖️ Forgetting to redeem codes — they expire fast, check weekly for new ones
