This guide covers everything: best weapons, artifact sets, team compositions, rotation tips, and whether her constellations are worth it.

🎯 Talent Priority

Mavuika's damage is heavily split between her Normal Attacks and her Elemental Skill. Her Burst is a team buff and utility tool, not her primary damage source.

  1. Normal Attack (★) — Her primary damage. Level to 10 as soon as possible.
  2. Elemental Skill (★) — Off-field damage and Pyro application. Also level to 10.
  3. Elemental Burst — Team buff and Nightsoul maintenance. Level 8-9 is fine.
💡 Resource tip: If you're resin-constrained, take NA and Skill to 8 each, then Burst to 6. Come back to crown them later.

🏆 Best Team Compositions

🔥 Mavuika Vaporize (Highest Single-Target Damage)

Mavuika + Furina + Kazuha + Bennett
Mavuika (Main DPS) · Furina (Sub-DPS/Buffer) · Kazuha (VV/Elemental DMG) · Bennett (ATK Buff/Heal)
The premium Mavuika team. Furina provides massive damage bonus and Hydro application. Kazuha shreds Pyro RES and adds DMG bonus. Bennett heals the Furina HP drain and provides ATK buff. Rotation: Furina E+Q → Kazuha Q+E → Bennett Q+E → Mavuika E → NA spam. Expect 80k-120k per NA with good gear.

🔥 Mavuika Melt (Speedrun King)

Mavuika + Citlali + Kazuha + Bennett
Mavuika (Main DPS) · Citlali (Cryo Shield/Res Shred) · Kazuha (VV/Pyro DMG) · Bennett (ATK Buff)
Melt damage is roughly 2x stronger than Vaporize for Pyro reactions. Citlali provides Cryo application, a shield, and -20% Pyro RES shred. This team has the highest burst screenshot potential in the game — Mavuika's skill can hit 200k-300k on Melt. Rotation: Citlali E+Q → Kazuha Q+E → Bennett Q → Mavuika E → NA.

🔥 Mavuika Mono-Pyro (Comfortable & Consistent)

Mavuika + Xiangling + Kazuha + Bennett
Mavuika (Main DPS) · Xiangling (Sub-DPS) · Kazuha (VV/Pyro DMG) · Bennett (Heal/ATK Buff)
No reaction management needed. Xiangling adds 30-40% team DPS with her burst. Triple Pyro makes energy a non-issue. Bennett burst + Kazuha VV is already enough to make Mavuika hit hard. Best against enemies that resist Hydro/Cryo. Rotation: Bennett Q → Kazuha Q+E → Xiangling E+Q → Mavuika E → NA.

🔥 Mavuika Overload (AoE Monster)

Mavuika + Chevreuse + Fischl + Bennett
Mavuika (Main DPS) · Chevreuse (Overload Support) · Fischl (Electro Sub-DPS) · Bennett (ATK Buff)
Chevreuse's C6 provides 60% Pyro/Electro DMG bonus and 40% RES shred for both elements. Overload triggers AoE damage. Excellent for multi-wave content. Rotation: Chevreuse E → Bennett Q → Fischl E → Mavuika E → NA. The constant Overload staggers most enemies, giving you free reign to attack.

⚡ Energy & Rotation Tips

  • Mavuika generates very little energy herself — she needs a battery. Bennett with Favonius Sword is ideal.
  • Her Nightsoul state lasts ~11s at C0. Use the full duration before swapping off.
  • Don't use her Burst mid-combo — it's for setup only. Pop it before entering Nightsoul state.
  • In co-op, Mavuika shines because she's self-sufficient. Just Bennett is enough to make her work.
  • Against weekly bosses like Arlecchino or the Narwhal, Mavuika's Nightsoul state bypasses most mechanical phases — she can deal damage continuously while other characters are dodging.

📝 Verdict

Mavuika is the definitive Pyro DPS as of v6.3. She out-damages Hu Tao, Arlecchino, and Yoimiya in most scenarios, has better AoE coverage, and more team flexibility. Her only weakness is energy generation — solved by Bennett + Favonius.

If you need a Pyro main DPS, she's the best pull you can make in v6.3. If you already have Hu Tao or Arlecchino built, Mavuika is still an upgrade but not mandatory — your existing Pyro DPS can still clear all content.

🎯 Final verdict: Mavuika is an S+ tier pull. Best Pyro DPS in the game, flexible team options, and future-proof Nightsoul mechanics. Recommended for any account lacking a strong Pyro carry.
