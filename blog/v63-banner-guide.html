Banner Pulling Guide v6.3 Primogems

Version 6.3 "Moon of Four" brings a stacked banner lineup with new Natlan characters and highly anticipated reruns. Here's who's worth your Primogems.

📋 Phase 1 Banners

Featured 5-Star: Mualani 🌊

Role: Main DPS (Hydro, Catalyst)

Mualani is a Natlan Hydro catalyst user who deals massive single-target damage through her Nightsoul-enhanced shark bites. Her charged attacks enter Nightsoul's Blessing, allowing her to surf across enemies while dealing Hydro damage.

  • Verdict: ✅ Strong pull if you need a Hydro DPS (or don't have Neuvillette)
  • Cons: Single-target focused, needs good team setup for vape
  • Best teammates: Xiangling, Kazuha, Zhongli
  • Weapon: Signature catalyst (CRIT Rate substat) — good but not must-pull

Rerun 5-Star: Kazuha 🍁

Role: Anemo Support / Sub-DPS

What can we say that hasn't been said? Kazuha is the best Anemo support in the game. He groups enemies, provides 40% elemental damage bonus at 1000 EM, shreds resistance with VV, and does respectable damage himself.

  • Verdict: ✅✅✅ MUST PULL if you don't have him. Top 3 character in the game.
  • Cons: C0 is already amazing. Constellations are luxury only.
  • Weapon: Freedom-Sworn is great but not necessary — Iron Sting (craftable) is 90% as good

📋 Phase 2 Banners

Featured 5-Star: Kinich 🌿

Role: Main DPS (Dendro, Claymore)

Kinich is a Natlan Dendro claymore user with an unique skill-based rotation. He enters Nightsoul's Blessing and can grapple around enemies while dealing Burning / Burgeon damage.

  • Verdict: ⚠️ Niche but powerful. Only pull if you like his playstyle.
  • Best teammates: Bennett, Emile, Baizhu, Thoma
  • Weapon: Signature claymore (DMG% substat) — significant upgrade over alternatives

Rerun 5-Star: Raiden Shogun ⚡

Role: Battery / Sub-DPS / Hypercarry

Raiden remains one of the most versatile characters in the game. She batteries the entire team, does respectable damage during her burst, and enables both Rational (Raiden National) and Hyperbloom teams.

  • Verdict: ✅ Strong pull for account flexibility
  • Cons: C0 is great but not "broken". C2 and C3 are massive spikes.
  • Weapon: Engulfing Lightning is very good. The Catch (R5) is F2P king.

💎 Pull Priority Ranking

  1. 🔴 Kazuha (if missing) — Best support in the game
  2. 🔴 Raiden Shogun (if missing) — Enables multiple team archetypes
  3. 🟡 Mualani — Strong Hydro DPS, good for vape teams
  4. 🟡 Kinich — Niche strong, needs investment

🔮 Future Predictions

Based on the rerun schedule, expect these characters in upcoming patches:

  • Xilonen — Expected soon. A 5-star geo support that could shake up the geo meta.
  • Nahida rerun — Overdue. Likely in v6.4 or v6.5.
  • Furina rerun — Already had one recently, but she's popular enough to see another sooner than expected.
  • Arlecchino rerun — Possibly in 6.4-6.5 given her popularity.
💰 F2P advice: You can guarantee one 5-star per patch cycle (42 days) if you do dailies, events, and exploration. If a banner has both a new character AND Kazuha, prioritize Kazuha — he will be meta-relevant for years.
