Meta Natlan Team Building v6.3

The release of Natlan has fundamentally changed the Genshin Impact meta. With the introduction of the Nightsoul mechanic and powerful new support characters, the optimal team compositions in v6.3 look very different from the Fontaine era.

🧩 What Changed in Natlan?

Three key mechanics define the Natlan meta:

  • Nightsoul's Blessing — Natlan characters enter a special state that deals extra damage and enables unique traversal abilities. Characters like Kachina, Mualani, and Kinich all rely on this mechanic.
  • Elemental Skill Focus — Many Natlan characters shift power away from burst-dependency, making them excellent in quickswap teams.
  • Support Reruns — Archons and other game-changing supports (Furina, Nahida, Kazuha, Zhongli) remain core to the meta, but their best teams now include Natlan DPS units.
📊 The v6.3 meta is a "two-tier" system: Your team either revolves around a top-tier 5-star carry (Neuvillette, Alhaitham, Arlecchino, Mualani) or a reaction engine (Hyperbloom, National). Both work — but the ceiling on 5-star carries has never been higher.

🥇 S-Tier Teams in v6.3

1. Neuvillette Hypercarry

Neuvillette + Furina + Kazuha + Zhongli — Still the most comfortable team for Abyss. Neuvillette's charged attack hits everything in range, Furina provides massive damage bonus, Kazuha groups and shreds, Zhongli makes you immortal.

2. Alhaitham Quickbloom

Alhaitham + Nahida + Raiden (EM) + Baizhu / YaoYao — Alhaitham triggers spreads while Raiden's skill enables hyperbloom seeds. Nahida provides consistent dendro application and EM share. This team does both single-target and AOE effectively.

3. Arlecchino Overload

Arlecchino + Chevreuse + Fischl + Bennett — Arlecchino's bond-of-life mechanic gives her unmatched front-loaded damage. With Chevreuse's 40% res shred for pyro and electro, this team deletes single-target bosses faster than almost anything else.

4. Mualani Vaporize

Mualani + Xiangling + Kazuha + Zhongli — Mualani's Nightsoul-enhanced shark bites hit for 200k-400k. Pair with Xiangling's consistent pyro application for big vaporize numbers. Kazuha groups, shreds, and buffs. Zhongli provides shield and res shred.

5. Kinich Burning / Burgeon

Kinich + Bennett + Emile + Baizhu / Thoma — Kinich's skill-based rotation spends time in Nightsoul state, triggering burning or burgeon reactions. Excellent for AOE chambers with grouped enemies.

🏆 Best 4-Star Only Teams

Not pulling for 5-stars? These teams still clear Abyss efficiently:

  • International Budget: Xiangling + Xingqiu + Bennett + Sucrose
  • Hyperbloom: DMC + Collei + Kuki / Lisa + Barbara
  • Aggravate: Fischl + Keqing (standard) + Sucrose + YaoYao
  • Mono-Pyro: Xiangling + Bennett + Sucrose + Pyro Traveler

🎯 What to Pull For in v6.3

If you're planning your pulls, here's the priority:

  • Must-pull supports: Furina, Nahida, Kazuha, Zhongli — these never expire
  • Top DPS: Neuvillette, Arlecchino, Alhaitham, Mualani
  • Skip: Standard banner characters, C1+ constellations unless you main them
💡 Final advice: The meta shifts every patch, but investing in universal supports (Furina, Nahida, Kazuha, Zhongli, Bennett, Xingqiu, Xiangling, Fischl) will never be a waste. Two well-built teams with these characters can clear any Abyss, regardless of the current blessing.
