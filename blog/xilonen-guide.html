Xilonen Build Guide Natlan Support v6.4

Xilonen is a 5-star Geo sword user from the Nanatzcayan tribe of Natlan. Since her release, she has established herself as one of the most versatile and powerful support characters in the entire game — often called the "Geo Kazuha" for her unique RES Shred mechanic that works even against elemental shields and immune enemies.

In v6.4's Nod-Krai meta, Xilonen remains a top-tier pick for reaction teams, mono-element compositions, and Abyss speed clears. Here's everything you need to build her optimally.

TL;DR: Xilonen is an S+ tier support for any team that wants RES Shred + healing + Nightsoul mobility. Build her with 4pc Scroll of the Hero of Cinder City, DEF% / DEF% / Healing Bonus or CRIT Rate, and prioritize her Skill > Burst.

🔍 Kit Overview

Xilonen's kit revolves around her Nightsoul's Blessing state, which she enters when using her Elemental Skill. During this state, she gains:

  • Rapid mobility — climbs walls and glides at high speed with low Nightsoul point consumption
  • RES Shred — nearby enemies have their resistance to the active character's element reduced by 36% at max talent level
  • Geo application — her Normal Attacks deal Geo damage and generate shards

Her Elemental Burst converts DEF into party-wide healing, making her a reliable sustain option for any team composition. At C0, she already delivers everything most teams need.

🛡️ Best Artifacts

4pc Scroll of the Hero of Cinder City (Recommended)

This is Xilonen's uncontested best set. The 2pc gives a 15% Geo DMG bonus, and the 4pc provides a massive 40% Elemental DMG bonus to the active character's element after triggering a Nightsoul-aligned reaction. Since Xilonen applies Geo constantly during her Skill state, this buff has near-100% uptime.

Alternative Sets (Situational)

  • 4pc Noblesse Oblige — If another teammate already has 4pc Scroll. Decent team ATK buff, but you lose Scroll's 40% DMG bonus.
  • 2pc Husk + 2pc Scroll — Transitional build while farming. Better personal damage, worse team buffs.
  • 4pc Archaic Petra — Niche for mono-Geo teams. Clunky to use but potent for Noelle/Itto comps.

👥 Best Team Compositions

🏆 S-Tier: Mualani Vaporize

Xilonen's RES Shred and Scroll buff make Mualani's nuke hits even more devastating. Rotation: Xilonen E > Kazuha Q > Xiangling E+Q > Mualani E+CA.

Team: Mualani + Xilonen + Kazuha + Xiangling

🏆 S-Tier: Neuvillette Hypercarry

Xilonen replaces Kazuha in this variant with better sustain. Her RES Shred works on all enemies Neuvillette hits, and she provides healing for Furina's fanfare drain.

Team: Neuvillette + Furina + Xilonen + Kazuha / Zhongli

🏆 S-Tier: Arlecchino Overload

Cinder City's 40% Pyro DMG bonus + Xilonen's 36% Pyro RES Shred = massive damage amplification. Chevreuse provides the healing and ATK buff.

Team: Arlecchino + Chevreuse + Xilonen + Fischl / Bennett

A-Tier: Mono-Geo

While Xilonen isn't as potent for Geo teams as Gorou, she still brings solid RES Shred and healing. Her mobility helps in the new Nod-Krai domains.

Team: Itto / Navia + Xilonen + Gorou + Furina / Zhongli

A-Tier: Clorinde Aggravate

Xilonen consolidates shred + healing into one slot, freeing the fourth slot for an offensive option. Fischl or Nahida work well here.

Team: Clorinde + Fischl + Nahida + Xilonen

🔢 Talent Priority

  1. Elemental Skill (E) — Highest priority. Most of your team support and Nightsoul utility scales with this. Level to 9+.
  2. Elemental Burst (Q) — Healing scales with DEF. Level to 8+ for comfortable sustain.
  3. Normal Attack — Skip. Only used during Nightsoul state for Geo application; damage is secondary.

Recommended investment: 1/9/8 (minimal), 1/10/9 (optimal). Crown the Skill if you main her.

⭐ Constellation Analysis

C1Extended Nightsoul duration + 20% team movement speed. Nice QoL but not mandatory.
C2+20% DEF, +20% Geo DMG for party. Solid damage boost.
C4Burst healing CRIT rate increases. Good for hybrid builds.
C6Nightsoul state grants 100% CRIT Rate + 60% CRIT DMG to the active character. Absolutely broken.

Verdict: C0 Xilonen is already a complete character. C2 is a meaningful spike. C6 turns her into one of the strongest buffers in the game. Save your primos unless you're a whale.

💬 Final Thoughts

Xilonen is the single most versatile support added in the Natlan cycle. She provides RES Shred, team-wide healing, Nightsoul mobility, and exceptional artifact set synergy — all from C0 with a 4-star weapon. If you skipped her first banner, her rerun is absolutely worth pulling for.

Compared to Kazuha: Xilonen trades grouping power for healing + better sustain. In v6.4's Nod-Krai domains where enemies are spread across large arenas, her mobility and raw shred often outperform Kazuha's crowd control.

Pull rating: ⭐ 10/10 — one of the best investments you can make for your account.
