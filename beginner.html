📖 Beginner's Guide

Roadmap · Resource planning · Must-build characters · Common mistakes

🗺️ Roadmap

📌 Adventure Rank 1-25 (Exploration Phase)

  • Follow the main story quests, unlock teleport waypoints and Statues of The Seven
  • Pick up Oculi when you see them, mark their locations (Anemoculus/Geoculus)
  • Daily commissions are a must (4 per day, main Primogem source)
  • Use resin on Ley Lines: EXP books + Mora
  • Don't farm artifact domains (waste of resin before AR 45)
  • Focus on 1-2 characters and level them to cap

📌 AR 25-35 (Transition Phase)

  • After world level increases, immediately farm boss ascension materials
  • Run talent domains and weapon material domains (rotates Tue/Thu/Sat etc.)
  • Start thinking about team comps — even though it doesn't need to be perfect yet
  • Weekly bosses: Stormterror and Wolf of the North
  • Don't spend Primogems on the standard banner! Only pull on limited character banners

📌 AR 35-45 (Growth Phase)

  • Keep leveling characters and talents
  • Craft 4-star weapons (Sapwood Blade/Amenoma Kageuchi etc.)
  • Start saving Condensed Resin (for artifact farming at AR 45+)
  • Clear Abyss up to floor 8-3 (to get Xiangling)
  • Start building a second team

📌 AR 45+ (Welcome to Artifact Hell)

  • Finally, gold artifacts are available!
  • Focus on getting correct main stats first, don't chase substats
  • Keep doing weekly bosses (Azhdaha/Raiden/Scaramouche etc.)
  • Attempt Abyss floors 9-12
  • Resin = Artifacts (unless character levels/weapons/talents aren't maxed)
💎 Resource Priority

🔑 How to Spend Primogems

  • Only pull on limited character event banners — Don't touch the standard banner!
  • Don't use Primogems for resin refresh (buying once a day is OK, but beginners should save)
  • Priority picks: Kazuha / Raiden Shogun / Nahida / Zhongli (universal T0 supports)
  • 4-star characters will all reach C6 eventually — no need to pull hard for them
  • Blessing of the Welkin Moon (Gnostic Hymn) is the best value purchase

⚡ How to Use Resin (Ranked Priority)

  • 1. Weekly Bosses — 3 half-price runs per week, must-do
  • 2. Character/Weapon Ascension Materials — Prioritize leveling to 80/90
  • 3. Talent Books — Get core skills to level 8
  • 4. Artifacts (after AR 45) — The endless grind
  • 5. Ley Lines — Run when you need Mora/EXP
  • 6. Weapon Ascension Materials — Only when needed
🌟 Must-Build 4-Star Characters

The following 4-star characters are worth building and irreplaceable in many teams:

  • Bennett — 6-star Pyro Archon! ATK buff + healing. NEVER C6 HIM!
  • Xingqiu — 6-star Hydro God! Best off-field Hydro application.
  • Xiangling — Free, off-field Pyro ceiling
  • Kuki Shinobu — Core of Hyperbloom, EM-scaling healer
  • Fischl — Sub-DPS Electro applier, core for Aggravate
  • Sucrose — Budget EM buffer, crowd control
  • Faruzan — Anemo specialist support (C6 game-changer)
  • Rosaria — Crit rate buffer, energy battery

⚠️ Common Mistakes
  • ❌ Don't spend Primogems on the standard banner — Use free blue Fates only
  • ❌ Don't farm artifacts before AR 45 — No gold drops, wasted resin
  • ❌ Don't C6 Bennett — Will limit many team comps
  • ❌ Don't level all characters evenly — Focus resources on one team first
  • ❌ Don't ignore Elemental Resonance — Double Pyro +25% ATK, Double Hydro +35% Healing
  • ❌ First 3 weekly bosses cost 30 resin — Always clear first 3 runs each week
  • ❌ Don't use Fragile Resin on normal materials — Save them for artifact farming at AR 45
🎯 Early Game Goals
  • One complete team — Main DPS Lv80 + weapon Lv80 + core talent Lv8
  • Hyperbloom — Nahida + Xingqiu + Kuki, cheapest T0 team
  • Raiden National — Raiden + Xingqiu + Xiangling + Bennett, one team clears Abyss
  • Monthly Abyss full clear — 600 Primogems × 2, stable income
  • 100% World Exploration — Chests + Oculi + quests give plenty of Primogems
