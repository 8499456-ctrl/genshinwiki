❓ FAQ

Is Genshin Impact free to play?

Yes! Genshin Impact is completely free to download and play. The game features optional in-app purchases for Primogems, Battle Pass, and Blessing of the Welkin Moon, but all content can be enjoyed without spending money.

Is there cross-save across platforms?

Yes! Genshin Impact supports cross-save (cross-progression) between PC, Android, iOS, and PlayStation. Link your HoYoverse account on all devices. Note: Cross-play between PlayStation and PC/mobile for co-op is fully supported.

How big is the download?

The initial installer is ~300 MB, but the full game download is approximately 85 GB on PC, 30 GB on mobile, and 75 GB on PS5. The total installed size will be larger after updates accumulate. Make sure you have enough free space and a stable internet connection.

Can I play Genshin Impact on Mac?

There is no official Mac client. However, you can play via cloud gaming services (GeForce NOW, Boosteroid) or use CrossOver / Parallels on Apple Silicon Macs. Mobile version runs well on iPad.

Is Genshin Impact on Nintendo Switch?

HoYoverse announced a Switch version but it has not been released as of June 2026. You can play on Switch 2 via cloud streaming services like GeForce NOW.
