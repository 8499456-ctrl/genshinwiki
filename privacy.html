Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 1, 2026

Genshin Wiki by Song ("we", "our", "us") operates the website genshin-wiki.pages.dev. This page explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you visit our site.

1. Information We Collect

We collect minimal information automatically:

  • Usage Data: Page views, referral links, browser type, device type, and operating system — used for analytics via Cloudflare Web Analytics.
  • Cookies: We use cookies only for core site functionality (e.g., remembering your login session and BGM playback preference). No third-party tracking cookies are used.
  • Account Data: If you register an account, we store your email address and a hashed password. We never store plain-text passwords.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To improve site content and user experience
  • To display ads relevant to you (via Baidu Union — see Section 5)
  • To enable member login and personalization features

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Cloudflare — For CDN, security, and basic web analytics. Cloudflare's privacy policy is available at cloudflare.com/privacypolicy.
  • Baidu Union (百度联盟) — For serving advertisements. Baidu may use cookies and device information to deliver relevant ads. Baidu's privacy policy is at union.baidu.com.

4. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal data we hold about you
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Disable cookies in your browser settings at any time
  • Opt out of personalized ads via optout.aboutads.info

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at: st.sqa@163.com

5. Advertising (Baidu Union)

This site uses Baidu Union to display advertisements. Baidu may use cookies and web beacons to collect information about your visits to this and other websites in order to provide targeted advertisements. You can learn more about Baidu's data practices in their privacy policy linked above.

6. Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your data, including HTTPS encryption, password hashing (SHA-256 with salt), and secure token-based authentication. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

7. Data Retention

We retain your account data until you request deletion. Usage analytics data is retained by Cloudflare for up to 6 months. Comment data is retained indefinitely for site functionality unless you request removal.

8. Children's Privacy

This site is rated 12+ and is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.
