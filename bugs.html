⚔️ Combat & Skills
Capitano's Burst — Skill not triggering below 40% HP Investigating
When Capitano's HP drops below 40% while his burst animation is active, the Cryo field may fail to deploy. The burst still goes on cooldown. Community reports suggest this occurs most frequently in co-op.
📅 Reported: Jun 28 🔄 Affected: v6.3 Capitano Burst Co-op
Columbina's Skill — Enhanced E DMG not applying after swap Confirmed
After swapping Columbina out and back in during her enhanced state, the increased Skill DMG bonus is lost. The visual effect (blue aura) persists but damage numbers revert to base. Restarting the rotation fixes it.
📅 Reported: Jun 25 🔄 Affected: v6.3 Columbina Skill
Vaporize damage bonus not applying to Charged Attacks with certain catalysts Workaround
Catalyst users (Mizuki, Mualani) sometimes fail to trigger the full 2.0x Vaporize multiplier on charged attacks. The issue correlates with high latency (>150ms). Switching to Normal Attack combos resolves it.
📅 Reported: Jun 20 🔄 Affected: v6.2+ Reactions Catalyst
Nightsoul Burst proccing twice from a single skill (Natlan) Confirmed
When using a Natlan character with Nightsoul Blessing active, the Nightsoul Burst can double-proc under certain conditions (overlapping frames with party healing). Provides unintended energy and DMG bonus. HoYoverse has acknowledged the issue.
📅 Reported: Jun 22 🔄 Affected: v6.2+ Natlan Nightsoul Exploit
🗺️ Exploration & World
Mizuki's Skill — unable to interact with certain terrain after landing Workaround
After using Mizuki's hold Skill to travel over gaps, landing on uneven terrain (rocky cliffs, tree roots) prevents interaction prompts (investigate, climb) from appearing for ~3 seconds. Sprint cancels the lock.
📅 Reported: Jun 27 🔄 Affected: v6.3 Mizuki Movement
Natlan Saurian — climbing stamina inconsistency Confirmed
The Tepetlisaur (Geo) consumes 30% more stamina than displayed when climbing crystalline walls. Only affects the Saurian form — the character climbing ability works correctly. HoYoverse is working on a fix.
📅 Reported: Jun 19 🔄 Affected: v6.0+ Saurian Natlan
"Moon of Four" domain — second wave enemies not spawning Fixed (Jun 29)
Players could not complete the new artifact domain because the second enemy wave failed to trigger after clearing the first. A hotfix was deployed on June 29. Restart the game if you still encounter this.
📅 Fixed: Jun 29 🔄 Affected: v6.3.0 only Domain Critical
🎨 Visual & Display
Grass textures flickering on iPhone 15 Pro Max Workaround
After the v6.3 update, grass shaders flicker aggressively on Apple A17 Pro chipsets. Reducing "Visual Effects" to Medium resolves the issue. Affects all mobile devices using MetalFX upscaling.
📅 Reported: Jun 24 🔄 Affected: v6.3 Graphics iOS
Character portrait not updating in party setup after ascension Fixed (Jun 27)
Ascending a character past level 60/70 did not update the portrait thumbnail in the party setup screen until a full game restart. Resolved in the v6.3 patch on Jun 27.
📅 Fixed: Jun 27 🔄 Affected: v6.2–6.3 UI Minor
Serenitea Pot — furnishings disappearing after placing a character Workaround
Assigning a companion to a seat can cause nearby furnishings (particularly small decor items) to become invisible. They return when you enter Edit mode. Avoid placing companions near clustered decor.
📅 Reported: Jun 15 🔄 Affected: v6.2+ Teapot Cosmetic
👥 Co-op & Multiplayer
Host migration crash when player disconnects during boss fight Investigating
If a co-op member disconnects during a world boss encounter (especially weekly bosses), the host's game may freeze for 5-10 seconds. In some cases, the boss's health bar desyncs from actual HP. No known workaround — avoid 4-player co-op on weekly bosses.
📅 Reported: Jun 21 🔄 Affected: v6.2+ Co-op Boss Crash
Puzzle progress not syncing for non-host players in Natlan Confirmed
In co-op mode, environmental puzzles in the new Natlan area (Flameheart Basin) may show as solved for one player but unsolved for another. The host's progress is always correct. Non-hosts should temporarily leave and rejoin to sync.
📅 Reported: Jun 26 🔄 Affected: v6.3 Co-op Natlan
